About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Chicago a licensed clinical therapist with 20 years of experience in a variety of therapeutic settings.

I draw from several methodologies and techniques to incorporate what is most helpful for each person.

I offer a highly personalized approach, tailored to each person’s individual needs in order to help them attain the personal growth they’re striving for.

Together with each client, I work to enhance each person’s strengths to help define and achieve their short term and life long goals.

I also provide LCSW supervision and consultation to recent social work graduates and provide individual & group consultation.

My office is located in downtown Evanston. Click on Direction tab for map.
