Relax - Reflect - Resolve

Welcome

Making the decision to seek a therapist is a brave decision and can be the greatest gift you can give to yourself at this time. Finding a therapist who you can connect with and one that you feel is a “good fit” is an important first step in this process.

In a supportive and confidential setting, I offer a relaxing space where clients can reflect and focus on personal growth and positive change.

I provide practical feedback to help clients address current life challenges and resolve long-standing issues. Often the goal of therapy can be as simple as becoming aware of patterns and understanding the motivations linked to behaviors.

I provide psychotherapy to individuals, school aged-children/teens, college students, couples, and families.

Whether you want support getting through a particularly difficult time or you are looking for a deeper exploration of your personal challenges and/or you are interested in an opportunity to improve your relationships, you can feel free to call me at 773.517.1661 for a phone consultation or click on my email anywhere on this web-site.
